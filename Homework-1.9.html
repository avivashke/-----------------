Pita Bread recipe

Try this simple recipe to make a bakery class Pita Bread that can be filled with lots of filling without breaking or tearing!


Ingredients

  • 3/4 White Flour
  • 1/4 Whole Flour
  • 3/4 Cup of Olive Oil
  • 2 cups of Water
  • 1 Spoon of Salt
  • 2.5 Spoons of Brown Sugar
  • 1 Spoon of Dry Yeast

Preperation

  1. Mix all the ingredients in the Food proccesor and mix for 7 minutes to stretch the Gluten
  2. Pour the dough into a big bowl and let it sit in the fridge covered with nylon for 3 hours
  3. Put a baking paper on the over trey and rub it with olive oil
  4. Put a handful size chunk of the dough on the trey and arrange with gaps between the different chunks
  5. Put in a pre-heated oven to 200 Degrees for 10 minutes
